Get to know Brian

I was born in Chicago, IL, and spent most of my childhood in the beautiful mountains of West Virginia where I enjoyed hiking, camping, and spending time with friends.

I called many places home, from Jacksonville, FL to Brooklyn, NY to Atlanta, GA. I settled in Charleston, SC in 2006 and now live on James Island with my lovely wife Helen and daughter Isabel.

Spending time with family is the most important thing to me. Together, we enjoy the sand at Folly Beach, playing at the park, riding bicycles, traveling to nowhere in particular, or just relaxing at home.

I traveled all over the globe for business and pleasure, learning about various cultures and meeting people along the way. I'm able to strike up a conversation easily and connect with people I meet. To me, no one is a stranger.
