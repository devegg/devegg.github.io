We are looking for a eCommerce Manager to join their remote team in Great Place, Planet Earth

The eCommerce Administrator is responsible for performing a variety of duties related to maintaining and updating our website, as well as other product databases, and sourcing quality graphics, content and product listings that will drive customer engagement and revenue. The eCommerce Administrator is responsible for managing product information, image processing, product data, Q&A and data imports, as well as keeping the online catalog organized. The eCommerce Administrator helps to ensure that search engine optimization (SEO) best practices are followed.

Adding/deleting products-sku’s and associated images, descriptions, prices, promotions, discounts, etc. Editing web page layouts. Adding Meta Tags to item pages to help generate generic web traffic. Detailed knowledge of back-end functionality including maintenance of product, order, inventory, customer, and other tables driving the web site. Ability to create reports using web data and configure web site functionality. Good customer service/sales skills when assisting customers in placing their orders.

  • Manage the product catalog and create product listings that are SEO and eCommerce friendly and monitor performance
  • Oversee shipping management platform to ensure efficient and timely order fulfillment
  • Keep the online catalog organized and ensure a high-quality standard is maintained.
  • Photo editing, writing product descriptions, uploading and managing products.
  • Track product sales and analyze data
