We are looking for a Webmaster to join their remote team in Great Place, Planet Earth

WonderfulWorkplace is looking for a skilled enough Webmaster to join their remote team in Great Place, Planet Earth. This is a full-time, contract position that will go on for 3 months, with the possibility for employment after further review and evaluation.

Although sometimes called upon to develop entire websites, the main responsibility of a webmaster is to keep website content and design fresh, publish new content, streamlining navigation, enhance user experience and overall website functionality for organizations.

The Webmaster should be a leader in training, who can effectively manage our organization’s web presence. The purpose of the position is to coordinate all maintenance & updating of our CMS websites, eCommerce stores, and also assist with executing UX/UI optimizations accordance with all digital marketing & SEO campaigns. Ensuring websites are functional, user-friendly, visually pleasing, and up to date.

Webmaster use their technical side to code, creative side to design and collaborative side to fix issues. With such varied skills, Webmaster are critical to an organization’s online presence, making them a valued asset.

Responsibilities/Duties:

  • Identifying and solving all page speed and design issues that negatively impact SEO scores.
  • Work closely with the team's content marketing plan to add and format content to website for maximum SEO optimization.
  • Develop and implement landing pages and micro-sites as per design and requirements.
  • Assist in creating Google Analytics goals for every department's initiatives that take place on our website and translate that data into actionable insights for department leaders.
  • Assist in the management of outside web development vendors.
  • Assist in the maintenance, management, and categorization of photography database.
  • Assist team members with basic computer and IT needs.
  • Testing websites to determine if functional and user-friendly
  • Monitoring and analyzing site performance
  • Updating website content and reviewing SEO
  • Perform manual functional and cosmetic testing.
  • Monitor site traffic performance.
  • Train key clients and users in how to use the site.
  • Monitoring website for problems.
  • Understanding the traffic at this site & being able to identify which content is most often viewed; adding tracking code or tracking tools as needed; reporting back to the documentation group so that the writers can update and correct the most heavily used content.
  • Monitor and analyze site performance (e.g. traffic, conversions).
  • Create custom analytic reports (we will train)
  • Review and analyze data from Google Analytics
  • Implements improved design, layout, and functionality of website.
  • Monitors usage analytics and implements strategies to improve search engine results.
  • Ability to assist developers with troubleshooting and resolving minor issues as needed.
  • Execute all duties in an efficient, organized, and timely manner.
  • Research, then formulate ways to enhances user experience and add value to the site
  • Update the company website, digital assets, and brand product catalogs on a regular basis
  • Post content on various websites, based on client direction and scheduling
  • Keep track of ongoing website projects.
  • Investigate and report any operational/technical issues
  • Establish and/or assist in setting up processes, update images, descriptions and marketing copy
  • Develop a protocol for continuous site QA, troubleshoot and resolve any issues
  • Site Monitoring: Regularly monitor and analyze site performance
  • Test websites across browsers, operating systems and devices whenever necessary
  • Debug pages, including fixing broken links and images
  • Provide recommendations on how to improve client websites in order to generate more conversions and a create a better user experience
  • Communicating with account coordinator and/or account executives to fulfill client requests
  • Domain procurement and management

Qualifications/Skills:

  • At least 2 Years’ Experience in Websites & eCommerce development
  • Working knowledge of programming languages and operating systems
  • Familiarity of web standards
  • Familiarity of website management tools
  • Familiarity of web analytics and SEO best practices
  • Competent with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Competent with content management systems
  • Competent with databases
  • Comfortable with small programming and scripting tasks
  • Passion for learning new skills
  • Strong troubleshooting and analytical abilities
  • Ability to think of creative solutions
  • Detail-oriented and reliable
  • Highly motivated and a self-starter
  • Excellent organizational skills and ability to prioritize multiple tasks and timelines
  • Client-oriented and ability to manage fluid processes with shifting priorities.
  • Having a great eye for design and photography
  • Must be able to resolve website related issues in an effective & timely manner
  • Capable of working alone & in collaboration with various departments, i.e. marketing, graphic design, etc.
  • Some SEO Experience Preferred but Not Required
  • Organized self-starter who can follow instructions and work independently with little direct supervision
  • Excellent written communication skills
  • Beginner to Intermediate computer skills, including, but not limited to...
  • Initiative and self-management; the passion and ability to dive into the work, prioritize appropriately, and accomplish tasks within established timeframes
  • Strong attention to detail; ability to apply care, thoroughness, and high quality to every task using a concern for all areas and people involved
