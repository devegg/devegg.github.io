Real Estate Assistant

We are looking for a Real Estate Assistant to join our team in Great Place, Planet Earth

Are you a dependable and diligent professional who's been looking for a great opportunity to grow in the exciting field of real estate? Do you want to work closely with a top real estate agent? Do you have a go-getter attitude and mentality, and want to be surrounded with like-minded people? Do you enjoy and take pride of being in a support role and are looking for a long-term position?

If your answer is YES to all of the above, then we want to hear from you!

Top Agent is looking for a Real Estate Assistant/Operations Manager to assist a growing team. This position is responsible for the overall front office management, the logistical support for team members; and coordination of all other internal and external relationships.

This position focuses on ensuring the Lead Realtor functions at a high level of productivity. Ensuring the Lead Realtor schedule runs smoothly is the top priority. This involves preparing and maintain files prior to meetings. The position requires high competencies in office organization and management, interpersonal relationship skills, planning meetings and managing schedules. In addition, the position requires an ability to manage multiple tasks, set priorities, take initiative, work independently and be flexible in order to fulfill both stated and implied responsibilities. The assistant must be professional with telephone etiquette, display the utmost discretion and have excellent customer relations skills.

Responsibilities/Duties:

  • Entering listings and sales in (MLS)
  • Order and manage all photos/videos of our listings of homes
  • Manage new client leads; populate and maintain online client database
  • Maintain an organized online file system
  • Managing calendar(s) and scheduling appointments
  • Coordinate property showings, open houses, appraisals, inspections, etc.
  • Scanning/copying/emailing/submitting various docs and forms
  • Run daily task list and execute for sellers and buyers
  • Manage files and ensure completeness
  • Create Facebook ads as needed
  • Creating campaigns for marketing programs
  • Ordering marketing products
  • Create and print flyers and marketing materials
  • Answering phones, taking messages
  • Follow up with attorneys, bankers, brokers, agents and other professional partners accordingly
  • Follow up with clients to collect all documentation to obtain a complete package

Qualifications/Skills:

  • Excellent customer service skills
  • Tech savvy
  • Proficient with MS Office, Google Docs/Drive and social media platforms
  • Exceptional organizational skills
  • Ability to work well under pressure
  • Ability to think fast, multi task and come up with solutions
  • Excellent interpersonal, written and communication skills
  • Ability to learn new tasks quickly
  • Organizational and Project Management abilities
  • Great ability to focus
  • Knows and worked with; CRM, MLS, Dotloop/DocuSign
