We are looking for a Photographer to join their remote team in Great Place, Planet Earth

We are seeking a 1099 independent contract real estate photographers for taking interior/exterior photos of residential and commercial real estate. We need photographers that realtors and homeowners are going to feel comfortable with because you will sometimes be working around homeowners. Every real estate shoot will be different, and sometimes you will have to deal with people. We need someone who is not only talented but understands we are in a customer service oriented field. The photographer needs to be open to the request of realtors and listen to what they want. It may not always be the best shot, but if they want it, find a way to deliver that to them and they can see for themselves later. Always listen to the client.

Responsibilities/Duties:

  • Travel to and from properties. We bring the work to you and we take care of the scheduling and service.
  • Provide onsite real estate style photography and create floorplan drawings.
  • Electronically transmit work at the end of each workday.
  • No post-production work required or permitted.
  • Meet with realtors at assigned properties to provide professional quality photos.
  • Display professionalism in communication with clients during a media shoot.
  • Effectively manage time to follow an appointment schedule. BE ON TIME
  • Travel to and from appointments
  • Part-time Independent contractor position 1099
  • Electronically submit photos at the end of each workday.
  • No post-production work required or permitted. We have an in house team that will manage all post-production of photography and videography

Qualifications/Skills:

  • Perform work as needed on a 1099 contract basis.
  • Previous photography experience.
  • Previous experience in a customer service or guest facing role.
  • Must have reliable automobile transportation and insurance.
  • Must have a smartphone, high-speed internet, and a personal computer.
  • Must be reliable, punctual, and capable of following an appointment schedule.
  • Must have the following professional photography grade equipment: Full Frame DSLR Camera, 18mm Wide Angle Lens, and Tripod (3ft-5ft Range of Expansion).
  • Previous real estate photography experience.
  • Tech savvy, and proficient with computers.
  • Comfortable working at a fast pace while on your feet most of the day.
  • Presentable and personable, understanding that customers deserve excellent service.
  • Strong communication skills, organized, and detail oriented.
  • Organized with an eye for detail.
  • Flexible schedule during weekdays and weekends.
  • Must have reliable transportation.
  • The following experience is preferred but not required: Drone video/photography, floor plan drawing, video walkthroughs.
