Digital Marketing

We are looking for a Digital Marketing Associate to join their remote team in Great Place, Planet Earth

The Digital Marketing Associate assists the marketing team with the back-end management of the website as well as the day-to-day executions of digital marketing tasks. You will work directly with the Marketing Director on tasks related to website maintenance, social media posting and scheduling, web promotions, social media advertising, Google Adwords, press, public relations, blogs, email campaigns, and perform other supporting tasks.

This is an ideal position for a motivated individual who wants a career in marketing and is looking to gain experience by executing across a large digital marketing strategy, including, but not limited to: Content Management, Lead Generation, SEO and Website optimization, Marketing Operations and Analytics.

This is an entry-level position, but applicants MUST have a working knowledge of digital media terminology and the basics of paid search and SEO.

Responsibilities/Duties:

  • Develop PPC campaigns (paid search, display, remarketing, etc.) including keyword research, landing page selection & analysis, and account structuring
  • Compile campaign specifics, oversee daily and monthly budgets, campaign reporting and develop client-facing documentation
  • Daily optimization of campaigns, including creative A/B testing, market research, bid optimization and analysis of keyword reports
  • Assist account team with report analysis, highlight insights, and regularly provide performance recommendations
  • Monitor the digital landscape and identify trends
  • Occasionally aid in resolution of monthly spend accounting/billing
  • Executing on tactics outlined within each strategy
  • Merging or cleaning subscriber lists
  • Setting up groups, tags, or segments in subscriber lists
  • Consulting on the creation or updating of the signup form process, unsubscribe process/email content
  • Campaign/automation flow creation, testing, scheduling
  • Producing a campaign calendar and/or campaign themes
  • Creating new templates
  • Keep the marking department organized:maintain and manage editorial calender's, product launch plans, cloud assets and program/project tracking.
  • Perform operational execution of social media, email marketing campaigns, workflows, blogs, articles, newsletters and more.
  • Assist in data gathering to measure Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), marketing trends and help generate marketing progress reports.
  • Assist with photo/video/graphics/ needs and editing.
  • Coordinate logistics for industry and regional trade shows.
  • Execute all tasks on the editorial calendar for social media, email, and other digital touchpoints.
  • Stay current on cutting-edge industry trends and news across all channels.
  • Collaborate with digital marketing team to distribute content, track content performance, and optimize content for SEO, specific channels, and target audiences.
  • Assist in maintaining editorial calendar utilized by organization wide marketing managers.
  • Research and obtain digital links for emails (client social media links)
  • Pull analytics reports for digital campaigns using Google Digital Analytics
  • Implement Google suite and Google Docs to streamline digital orders and campaigns
  • Pull DFP reports for campaigns weekly and at the end of each campaign
  • Pull DFP reports to track inventory weekly
  • Work with multiple vendor dashboards for reporting weekly on campaigns
  • Updating website content (to accommodate changes in product pricing and information based on profit center’s needs.
  • Work and coordinate with website developers to ensure website functionality.
  • Ensuring all KPIs are being effectively tracked & reported for all marketing initiatives.
  • Staying current on SEM/SEO practices and collaborating with peers on the Digital Marketing team.
  • Maintains a detailed digital content calendar.

Qualifications/Skills:

  • A strong interest in digital media advertising and PPC
  • Experience with Google AdWords, Analytics, Bing Ads, and Facebook Ads Manager (or a strong willingness to learn)
  • Google AdWords Certification or willing to achieve within 30 days of hire
  • SEO/SEM experience
  • Experience with Klaviyo, Constant Contact, Mailchimp, Remarkety and/or Bronto preferred.
  • Strong skills in analysis, critical thinking, problem solving
  • Excellent communication (written, verbal) and organizational skills
  • Self-motivated, independent, proactive
  • Exceptional attention to detail
  • Experience in e-commerce preferred
  • Highly organized, pro-active and analytical.
  • Project management skills, Trello software experience a plus.
  • Strong interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to work in a fast paced environment.
  • Photography skills.
  • Skilled with MS Office including Microsoft Excel, Word and Outlook
  • Strong attention to detail
  • Strong analytical ability
  • The ability to meet strict deadlines, while maintaining accuracy
  • Comprehensive understanding of digital assets and current digital trends
  • Must be a confident, flexible, be able to multitask, and be able to work independently
  • The ability to effectively communicate, build rapport, and relate well to all kinds of people
  • Familiarity with Salesforce CRM, Hubspot, and WordPress preferred
  • Independent self-started with a positive attitude. Follow-through and responsiveness is also very important.
  • A deep understanding of how to collect and analyze data.
  • A mindset that allows you to understand industry trends and how they best fit our customer needs.
  • Relentless creativity and ambition when it comes to problem solving.
  • Attention to detail, excellent organizational skills, and superior time management skills.
  • Graphic/video production skills a plus.
